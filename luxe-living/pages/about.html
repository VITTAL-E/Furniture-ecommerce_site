Our Story

Crafting Comfort Since 2009

Founded in a small workshop in Brooklyn, Luxe Living started with a simple mission: to create furniture that combines timeless design with modern comfort. What began as a passion project by two furniture artisans has grown into a beloved brand trusted by thousands of homeowners.

Every piece in our collection is thoughtfully designed, using sustainably sourced materials and handcrafted techniques passed down through generations. We believe your home deserves furniture that tells a story.
